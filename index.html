Mutar Pilao

This easy peas pulao is a vegan and gluten-free dish that’s great to enjoy as a light meal or hearty side. Matar pulao features rice and tasty peas with onions and aromatic spices and can be prepared entirely in a pressure cooker or in a pan on a stovetop. I share three ways to make this delish matar pulao – stovetop pressure cooker method with stepwise photos, while the pan and Instant pot method with detailed instructions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp shredded ginger
  • 2 cups shelled peas
  • 2 tsp coriandar powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Water

Instructions

  • Wash the basmati rice well.
  • Soak it in warm water for an hour.
  • Strain the rice and set aside.
  • Heat the ghee in a heavy pen,and fry the cumin seeds in ginger
  • Add the peas,garam masala,salt,turmeric and rice and stir well.
  • Saute for about a minute.
  • Pour and sour cups of water,and allow it to boil.
  • Then ,lower the flame and allow the mixture to simmer.
